# 🐶 PetShop Management System

This project was developed by our team using **C#.Net**. It is a desktop application designed to help pet shops manage their daily operations more easily and efficiently.

---

## 🛠️ Business Process

The system supports different activities in running a pet shop, such as:

- Adding new products to the inventory  
- Editing and deleting existing products  
- Managing employee records  
- Managing customer information  
- Generating bills for customer purchases  

---

## 💡 System Features

### 🐾 Product Management
- Add, update, and delete products  
- Keep track of product details and inventory

### 👨‍💼 Employee Management
- Add and manage employee information  
- Store data like name, contact, and position

### 🧍 Customer Management
- Add and manage customer details  
- View purchase history and contact info

### 🧾 Bill Generation
- Create bills after a customer purchases items  
- Show item names, prices, and total amount to pay

---

## 📌 Overview

This PetShop Management System is made to make pet shop tasks simple and organized. It helps both employees and customers by making processes faster and more accurate.
